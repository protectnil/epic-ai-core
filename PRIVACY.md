# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 2, 2026
**Product:** Epic AI® Legion
**Contact:** privacy@protectNIL.com

---

## The Short Version

Epic AI does not collect, store, transmit, or process your personal data. Legion runs on your infrastructure. Your data never reaches Epic AI servers.

---

## What We Collect

**Nothing.**

Epic AI® Legion is an Intelligent Virtual Assistant (IVA) that classifies intent, selects adapters, calls them, and synthesizes a response. It runs entirely on your infrastructure. Epic AI has no servers in the data path between your host and the third-party APIs you connect to.

### Specifically, we do not collect:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, IP address)
- API keys or credentials you configure in Legion
- Prompts, queries, or responses passing through the gateway
- Usage metrics, telemetry, or crash reports
- Device identifiers or location data
- Any content from connected third-party services

---

## Third-Party Services

When you configure an adapter in Legion, your host connects **directly** to that third-party API (Salesforce, Slack, GitHub, etc.). Those requests originate from your infrastructure, not from Epic AI. Each third-party service's own privacy policy governs that data exchange.

---

## Contributor Communications

If you open a GitHub issue, submit a pull request, or contact us at our published email addresses (security@epic-ai.io, privacy@protectNIL.com), we receive whatever information you include — your GitHub username, email address, and message content. We use this only to respond to you. We do not sell it, share it, or use it for any other purpose.

---

## npm Package Downloads

Installing `@epicai/legion` via npm is logged by npm, Inc. (npmjs.com) as an anonymous download count. Epic AI does not receive identifying information from these logs. See [npm's privacy policy](https://docs.npmjs.com/policies/privacy) for details.

---

## GitHub

The source code for Epic AI Legion is hosted on GitHub. GitHub may collect analytics on repository visitors under [GitHub's privacy policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement). Epic AI does not receive or control this data.

---

## Children's Privacy

Our products are developer tools not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a material way, we will update the effective date and note the change in the repository changelog. Because we collect no data, material changes would only occur if we introduced a new data-collecting feature — which would be announced explicitly.
